### ðŸ§  John Rawls: Justice as Fairness
John Rawls is a heavy hitter here. He suggests we imagine a "Original Position" where we don't know if we are rich or poor. From there, we would choose these principles:

1.  **Equal Liberty:** Everyone gets basic rights (speech, freedom).
2.  **The Second Principle (for Inequalities):**
    * **Difference Principle:** Inequality is okay *only if* it helps the **poorest** people.
    * **Fair Opportunity:** Everyone should have an equal shot at success.

> **Essential Concept:** Rawls believes **Self-Respect** is the most important "social good" because without it, we lose the motivation to pursue a good life.
